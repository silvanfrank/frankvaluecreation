Strategic Framework

Owner Strategy

Is our business optimal in terms of risk and rewards compared to other shareholder investment opportunities? What are the expectations of the principal?

Business Strategy

Where and against whom and how/with what do we compete? What makes us valuable? Do we want to differentiate? Where and how do we want to grow?

Corporate Strategy

Which businesses are we in (long term view)? What resources do we allocate to those businesses? Which synergies do we strive for? Which alliances?

Configuration Strategy

How do we configure the 'Value Added'-Engine? Where are areas of freedom and where do we seek for synergies? How are we organized in terms of processes and structures?

Functional Strategies

What are the guiding principles and standards for our businesses with regard to general business functions?

Case Study: Birchmeier Group

The Challenge

Family business in 3rd generation with 600 employees and CHF 250 million revenue. Grown rapidly in 10 years from traditional construction company to complete provider including GU/TU, real estate development, high-rise, civil and special foundation engineering, building materials and transport logistics. Unsatisfactory profitability for 2 years.

The Solution

Development of comprehensive strategy by January 2026, establishment and moderation of strategy process by external consultant, content development of strategy independently by Birchmeier. Preparation for handover to 4th generation.
